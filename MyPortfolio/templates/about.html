My Story

Hello! I'm Sanjeet Thapa, a 21-year-old passionate developer from the beautiful hills of Nepal. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Information and Communication Technology Education (BICTE) at Birendra Multiple Campus, I'm on an exciting journey to blend technology with creativity.

🚀 My Development Journey

My adventure in technology began with a simple question: "How do websites actually work?" This curiosity led me down the rabbit hole of web development, where I discovered my true passion for backend development. I love architecting robust, scalable systems using Django and Python, and I'm constantly amazed by how clean code can solve complex real-world problems.

🤖 AI & Machine Learning Enthusiast

The future belongs to intelligent systems, and I'm actively expanding my knowledge in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I believe these technologies hold the key to solving some of humanity's biggest challenges, and I want to be part of that solution.

👨‍💼 Entrepreneurial Spirit

Beyond coding, I'm an entrepreneur at heart. I founded and actively run Aroha Wears, a fashion brand that has taught me invaluable lessons about business strategy, digital marketing, customer relations, and the importance of user experience - skills that directly complement my tech expertise.

🎸 Creative Balance

When I'm not coding or running my business, you'll find me with my guitar. Music is my creative outlet and mental reset button. There's something magical about how melodies and algorithms both follow patterns, and I often find that musical creativity sparks innovative solutions in my programming projects.
